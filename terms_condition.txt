Other tariffs or tariffs imposed by government or any other authority on the Services from time to time. Therefore, bear

Customer value-added tax, sales tax and/or service tax or any other taxes charged under

The tax law or any other applicable law or system according to the prevailing price and the customer must pay its value

Each party is solely responsible for tax payment obligations and filing obligations related to special income tax.*

The client warrants that all legal documents and data provided by him are accurate and correct. It also guarantees and bears responsibility for the contents of its shipment and its compliance with regulations and laws. *

. All necessary attempts are made to make the delivery process successful, and the shipment is returned after making all necessary attempts *

. The shipment does not stay for more than 10 days if it is not received and is returned to the supplier and can be returned earlier if the supplier requests it to be returned *

The actual weight of the shipment is up to 10 kg or the volumetric length * width * height /4000 (whichever is greater) *

We share the client's financial operations through his account on the website. It is done electronically, with the customer's right to object within a period not exceeding 15 days from the process *
The funds received after clearing are transferred according to the agreement between the two parties (immediate - weekly - semi-monthly - monthly *

In the event that the shipment is lost or completely damaged after receiving it and the reason is from the method of transportation or the representative’s handling of the shipment, a compensation value of 300 riyals shall be incurred, or the actual value of the shipment if its value is less *

* The customer undertakes not to use the service for any illegal, immoral, obscene or fraudulent purposes or for any

Other purposes prohibited by regulations on the map, country of origin, country of destination, or any other regulations. The customer also undertakes that

Any use of the Service is in compliance with all international, federal, state and local laws.

These laws include, but are not limited to, laws relating to banking, money laundering, commercial sanctions and terrorist acts

. يتم اجراء كل المحاولات اللازمة لإنجاح عملية التوصيل ، ويتم ارجاع الشحنة بعد اجراء كل المحاولات اللازمة *

يتم تحويل الأموال المستلمة بعد اجراء المقاصة حسب الاتفاق بين الطرفين ( فوري – أسبوعي – نصف شهري – شهري *

يكون أي استخدام للخدمة متوافقاً مع كافة القوانين الدولية والاتحادية وقوانين الولايات والقوانين المحلية.
